Tofu Tacos

Spicy tofu and fresh greens, with a vegan avocado crema.

A healthy helping of plant-based power for you today: Tofu Tacos. Lightly spicy, crumbly (in a good way), and packed with protein and fiber, these tasty tacos are ready in less than 30 minutes.

You can use the filling in all kinds of yummy ways beyond a tortilla, and it has a moderately crispy, pleasing texture that will make these tacos a hit even amongst tofu skeptics.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • taco seasoning
  • 1 block extra-firm tofu
  • 1 cup finely-shredded cabbage
  • 2 tablespoons apple-cider vinegar
  • hot sauce
  • handful of cilantro
  • 1 cup avocado
  • 1/3 cup coconut milk
  • lime juice
  • salt and pepper
  • small tortillas

Steps

  1. In pan, heat olive oil over medium until glossy. Crumble tofu and add soy sauce and taco seasoning packet.
  2. Reserve Some Cilantro. Toss the rest with and the cabbage, apple cider vinegar, and hot sauce in medium bowl.
  3. Blend avocado, coconut milk, and lime juice in small mixing bowl. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  4. When tofu is hot and slightly browned, remove from heat. Allow to cool and warm tortillas.
  5. Top tortillas with crumbled tofu, cabbage mix, and a spoonfull of avocado crema. Top With Cilantro and serve hot.
